ITMP

ITMP is an intelligent traffic management platform, which includes the traffic monitoring and analysis system, traffic congestion management system and traffic strategy prediction system. ITMP is based on the data mining and data visualization in traffic big data field to solve the traffic problems in cities.

I was in charge of data cleaning, GPS data processing and data visulization. And I implemented this Shanghai Traffic Monitoring and Analysis System demo based on Angular2 and D3.js. Here are several screenshots I can present.

THE INDEX PAGE

The index page shows the overview of the traffic conditions of the bus, taxi and subway at the moment. Various charts make the values and changing of data more intuitive and understanding.

THE BUS PAGE

The bus page shows the all bus routes in the city. User can choose a specific route and its stop to see the more detailed information.

THE TAXI SCREEN

The taxi page uses the heat map to show the taxi distribution of each area. It also allows users to select a specific area for further searching.

THE SUBWAY SCREEN

The subway page shows the all subway routes in Shanghai and lists the top 5 stations with highest passenger flow volume. Users can choose any subway route or station for detailed information.
