Eyegic

Eyegic is an augmented reality textbook which integrates text phonetics, picture browsing, video and audio watching, 3D models interactive operations, Chineses character writing and other functions. My team developed it by using Vuforia, Unity3D, 3ds Max and Xcode. Also, it includes the new designing textbook.

I was in charge of realizing Chinese charaters recognition and implementing 3D models interactive operations. Also, I reseached on target recognition and tracking algorithms in augmented reality to optimize the recognition accuracy.

The AR camera has seven functionalities, including text, audio, pictures, video, 3D models watching and operations. With these expanded information, the children can learn more from our application.
