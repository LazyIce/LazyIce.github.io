Ingenuity

Ingenuity is an e-commercial website aiming to show and sell handicraft works from ingenious handicraftsmen so that we can help to protect and inherit traditional cultural inheritance. And we combined the Chinese style and e-commercial style.

We used SpringMVC + Spring + Hibernate framework with Maven to implemented the website. I was in charge of actualizing the UI with AngularJS, designing and managing the MySql database and realizing the shopping seckill controllers and services.
