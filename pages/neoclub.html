Neoclub

Neoclub is an application focused on video presentations and startup teams founding to help business incubation. Entrepreneurs, investors, job huntes are connected to each other based on skill tabs and industry tabs.

I was in charge of implementing the IOS app UI of social circle module and articles sharing module. Also, this program was an entrepreneurial practice, and I learned much in financing and business plan.

Neoclub Exploration

In neoclub, there are investors, entrepreneurs and job hunters. Entrepreneurs can make online meeting appointments with investors and recruit staffs. Investors can hunt for the promising startup business. Job hunters can tag their skills to find the suitable jobs. All the users are connected together with their skill tags and work area tags.
